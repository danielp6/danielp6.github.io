Sales by Console

The answer is not that people are playing less video games than before, but the fact that they are playing them in a different way. The original iPhone was released in 2007, but it did not immediately get looked at as a way to play games. But as the developers got better at creating games, and found new ways to get profits off of them, including downloadable content and ad based revenue, more and more people began playing games on their phones and ipads.

Looking at the chart, all of the consoles up until XBox one (XOne) are non-mobile. The consoles from 3DS to the right are all the mobile consoles. If you hit the select year button, and select the year 2008, you can see that the DS was the second leading console for games sold. This shows that people were wanting to play mobile games, which made it the perfect time for phone gaming to start picking up some of the market share.
