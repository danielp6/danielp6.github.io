Sales by Manufacturer

As gaming has changed over the years, the major manufacturers have adapted to compete with the new trends. Consoles have embraced the downloadable content that has become so popular. New consoles from Microsoft and Sony are aiming to be looked at as more than video game machines, and more of a full entertainment system.

Since Sony released the original Playstation in 1994, they have been a dominant force in gaming. This is seen in the data below, as Sony holds the #1 spot on the sales list since 1980, but Nintendo is a very close #2. Nintendo is finding different ways to compete, with its innovative and creative ways to change the way we play video games. Since Nintendo released the Wii in 2012, it has seen a comeback in sales, because it offers an experience that cannot be had on another system, or a phone for that matter.
